CUSTOMER SERVICE DEPT

Operations Executive

Bridgeway
wants YOU !

Operations Executive (Click)

Description:   Your responsibilities will include acquiring new clients for the bank , highlighting our different products and their benefits to customers during marketing campaigns, and maintaining relationships with existing customers. You will also be available to assist other departments when staff strength is lacking.

Job Requirements:   The ideal applicant will have:-

  • At least 2 years of work experience as a sales person.
  • an Outgoing personality ,be able to connect with people and negotiate
  • Positive attitude,team spirit and a willingness to learn
  • OND/HND qualifications
  • WASSCE /NECO results with a minimum of 5 credits

Training & Benefits:  On this role, you will have access to the following:-

  • 22 days of paid work leave annually
  • Health insurance
  • Monthly marketing training, opportunity to progress to a managerial position after 2 years.
