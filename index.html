Frequently Asked Questions

What do I have to do get a loan ?

To get a loan you must possess at least 30% of the amount you require, in your bank account. You must come in with a your BVN, two passport photographs , and a valid form of identification(NIN slip , drivers license etc). Once these conditions are met you simply fill out a loan form and wait for a period of two weeks for the loan to be proccessed.

Can the bridgeway ATM card be used with any ATM machine ?

The bridgeway Issued card can be used at any ATM across the country. There are no restrictions. There is however a N35 for all withdrawals, which go to the owners of the respective ATMs.

What is your interest rate for savings accounts ?

The current interest rate on savings accounts is 3% annually .This is subject to regulatory changes by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN).
